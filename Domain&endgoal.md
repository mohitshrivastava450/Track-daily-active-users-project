# Domain & End Goal

## Domain: Product Analytics

Understanding how users interact with a product to make better decisions and drive growth.

**Key Focus Areas:**
- User behavior and engagement
- Feature adoption and usage
- User retention and churn
- Performance metrics and trends

---

## End Goal: Track Daily Active Users (DAU)

Build a system to track, monitor, and analyze how many unique users engage with the product each day.

### What is DAU?
The number of unique users who interact with the product in a single day.

### Why It Matters
- Indicates product health and growth
- Helps identify trends and problems
- Guides product decisions
- Measures success of new features

### Main Objectives

1. **Real-Time Monitoring** - Track DAU daily with up-to-date dashboards
2. **Trend Analysis** - Understand DAU patterns and growth
3. **Segmentation** - Break down DAU by user groups, geography, device
4. **Prediction** - Forecast future DAU and identify at-risk users
5. **Insights** - Provide actionable recommendations to increase engagement
